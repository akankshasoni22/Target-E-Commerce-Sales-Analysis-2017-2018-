# Target-E-Commerce-Sales-Analysis-2017-2018-
SQL-based analysis of Target's e-commerce sales data to identify customer trends, revenue growth, delivery performance, and business opportunities.


## Project Overview

This project analyzes Target's e-commerce sales data from 2017 to 2018 to understand customer behavior, sales trends, payment preferences, freight costs, and delivery performance across different states in Brazil.

The analysis was performed using SQL and focuses on generating business insights that can help improve sales performance, customer acquisition, logistics efficiency, and operational decision-making. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## Business Objectives

- Analyze customer distribution across states
- Study yearly and monthly sales trends
- Identify seasonal demand patterns
- Evaluate freight and delivery performance
- Understand customer payment behavior
- Generate actionable business recommendations

## Dataset

The dataset consists of multiple e-commerce tables:

## Key Findings

### Sales Growth

- Orders increased significantly from 2017 to 2018.
- Revenue increased by approximately 136% between January–August 2017 and January–August 2018. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

### Seasonal Trends

- Highest sales occurred during March, April, May, July, August, and November.
- Lowest sales were observed in September and October. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

### Customer Distribution

- Most customers were located in SP, RJ, MG, and RS states.
- Lowest customer concentration was found in AC, AP, and RR. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

### Time of Purchase

- Most orders were placed during the afternoon.
- Dawn recorded the lowest order volume. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

### Freight Analysis

- SP generated the highest total freight value.
- RR had the highest average freight cost per order. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

### Delivery Performance

- SP showed the fastest delivery service.
- RR recorded the slowest average delivery time. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

### Payment Behavior

- Credit Card was the most preferred payment method.
- UPI was the second most popular payment option. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

## Business Recommendations

- Launch targeted campaigns during low-performing months (September and October).
- Increase customer acquisition efforts in low-performing states.
- Improve logistics efficiency in states with longer delivery times.
- Use payment behavior insights to optimize checkout experiences.
- Introduce special promotions during low-demand time periods. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
